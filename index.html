Collage Web App

You will see a pop-up appear on your screen. Please immediatly press allow after you see the pop-up. It will come after you initiate a command.

Instructions:

1. Press start. 2. Get into a selfie pose. 3. Say, "Selfie," when you finally want to take the selfie. 4. You can then make a collage out of all of your selfies.
